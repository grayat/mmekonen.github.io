Welcome

Enver Creek Secondary is one of the best schools in Surrey, BC.We provide opportunities for success and excellence as we prepare students to become responsible members of the community.

Math

Our school provides opportunities to learn a variety of basic numeracy skills in the junior grades and also advanced university math in senior courses.

The staff at Enver is always committed and looking forward towards helping students succeed in math.

Languages

The language program at our school has the intention to train students to communicate in main languages and also develop an open choices to cultural diversity.

Science

It is impractical living our lives without science... Just imagine, we would have no electricity, internet, medicine.. and so on.

Our schools Science department strongly encourages students to be creative and be able to work with open-ended problems, the next generation of scientists, doctors, and engineers are in our hands.
